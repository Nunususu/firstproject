How to draw a rat

Click to see finshed rat

Start with an small circle

Now make two lines that angle outward

The next step is to draw two semi circles, these will be the ears

The head is a curved line

for the body draw two slightly curved lines, one starting at the ear, the other at the bottom of the snout. The second one by the snout should have a small "<" at the bottom

The second leg with be a semi circle with a "<" at the bottom

to close the body draw a upside down arch inbetween the feet

The tail will be two long "s" and lines for detail

finally the face, draw a circle near the right ear and two lines on the snout

now you have a rat
